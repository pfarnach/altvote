AltVote is a simple platform to help you make ranked choice ballots that anyone can vote on

  • Instead of only choosing one candidate, you get to rank your choices. This gets rid of the Spoiler Effect
  • To win, a candidate has to get more than 50% of the vote (unlike the First Past the Post system)
  • If no candidate has over 50% of the vote after the first round, the last place candidate gets eliminated -- whoever voted for them gets their vote redistributed to their second choice.
