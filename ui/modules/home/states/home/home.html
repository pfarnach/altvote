VoteGoat

VoteGoat is a simple tool to help you make ranked choice ballots that anyone can vote on

What's a ranked choice ballot? (aka alternative vote)
The quick and dirty:
  • Instead of only choosing 1 candidate, you get to rank your choices -- that's right, more choices! This eliminates the Spoiler Effect
  • To win, a candidate has to get more than 50% of the vote (unlike the First Past the Post system most elections use)
  • If there's no majority after the first round of counting, the last place candidate(s) get eliminted and their votes get redistributed to their votes' second choices
